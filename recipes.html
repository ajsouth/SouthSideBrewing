South Side Amber

▾

Ingredients

  • 9 lb Pale Malt
  • 1 lb Crystal 60L
  • 0.5 lb Munich
  • 1 oz Hallertau (60)
  • Yeast: American Ale

Process

  1. Mash 152°F for 60 minutes.
  2. Boil 60 minutes with hops additions at 60/30/5 min.
  3. Cool, ferment at 68°F until final gravity.

Tasting

Caramel notes, medium body, sessionable at ~5.4% ABV.

Rust Belt Haze (Hazy IPA)

▾

Ingredients

  • 10 lb Pale Malt
  • 1 lb Flaked Oats
  • 1 lb Wheat Malt
  • Hops: heavy late additions (example)
  • Yeast: NEIPA strain

Process

  1. Low-temperature mash, high hop aroma with hopstand.
  2. Dry hop heavily for aroma.

Tasting

Cold Corner Lager

▾

Ingredients

  • 9.5 lb Pilsner Malt
  • 0.5 lb Light Munich
  • Hops: Saaz
  • Yeast: Lager strain

Process

  1. Step mash or infusion at lager temps.
  2. Ferment cool and lager for several weeks.

Tasting

Crisp, clean, refreshing.
